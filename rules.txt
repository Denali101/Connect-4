Game - Connect 4: 
Try to build a row of four discs while keeping 
your opponent from doing the same!
Objective: 
Be the first player to get four of your coloured discs 
in a horizontal, diagonal, or vertical line.
How to Play: 
This is a two player game. Game alternates between players 
until one makes a row of 4 of their discs.
How to Win: 
Be the first to make a line of four of your discs!
